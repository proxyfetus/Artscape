About the artist

Helena Foster, (b.1988 Benin City, Nigeria) is an artist that lives and works in London. Foster's paintings seek to capture the day-to-day conflicts and complexities that engulf every household. Working with photography as her starting point, her works are largely direct studies from Nollywood cinema, where she freeze-frames films and paints the characters, giving them new meaning in the context of painting.
