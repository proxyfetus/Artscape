Press Release

‘Niels Corfitzen has developed a one-of-a-kind style of portraiture, landscapes, and figurative painting. It is characterized by multiple elements that catch the eye, making the results always fascinating. The portraits, specifically, have an exciting approach to present this long-known medium in a new, fresh light. What the viewer imagines as a correct portrait work would be the precision to present every detail in accordance with reality. Niels Corfitzen, with confident paint strokes, creates glitches that give the paintings a superb contemporary approach. People are the common theme of Niels Corfitzen’s paintings as he is passionately interested in human nature – the physique and psyche. As much as his early works contained a great study of detail, the literal and understandable, a shift towards abstract and incomprehensible can be noticed. This change allows room for imagination and subjectivity. It is a daring approach of the artist with all the respect to the viewer because it becomes a subject for interpretation and possible dialogue, rather than a given finale.
Art historian and specialist Peter Kær perfectly captures with words what Niels Corfitzen paints on the canvas: “Niels Corfitzen wants to move us. He invites us to begin a journey on multiple levels. We travel in a concrete sense. We travel through the memories, where we can adjust reality. We travel in dreams, where we can go anywhere. By sea. By land. Soaring through the air. Tuscany. Japan. A lift in the north of the city. Sitting, floating, or sitting mid-air on a rug surrounded by water and lily pads.”

About the artist

Niels Corfitzen (b. 1980) Copenhagen based, self-taught artist. His paintings have been shown in many group exhibitions since 2008. In 2018 he was commissioned a portrait of H.R.H. Crown Prince Frederik unveiled at Amalienborg. ‘Reflections’ - his first solo show, currently open for visitors to view at Galleri Christoffer Egelund (an art gallery focusing on young Scandinavian and international contemporary art).
