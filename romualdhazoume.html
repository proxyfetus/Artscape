About the artist

Romuald Hazoumè was born in 1962 in Porto Novo, Benin, where he lives and works. Romuald Hazoumè’s body of work primarily encompasses sculpture, but also painting, video and photography. His work is included in public collections including The British Museum, Musée Barbier-Mueller, Geneva Fondation Zinsou, Cotonou, Queensland Art Gallery, amongst others. Hazoumè has participated in the Biennale de Lyon and Gwangju Biennale (both 2000), as well as the 3rd Biennale of Contemporary Art at the Garage Museum for Contemporary Art, Moscow (2009). He was awarded the Arnold Bode-Prize for his participation in in documenta 12 (2007).
